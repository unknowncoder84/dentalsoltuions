# Image Setup Instructions

Please save your dental service images to the `public/images/` folder with the following exact names:

## Service Images (for Services Section)
1. `teeth-cleaning.jpg` - The teeth cleaning image with dental tool
2. `smile-designing.jpg` - The smile designing before/after image
3. `teeth-filling.jpg` - The teeth filling 3D model image
4. `root-canal.jpg` - The root canal treatment stages image
5. `teeth-whitening.jpg` - The teeth whitening before/after comparison
6. `wisdom-tooth.jpg` - The wisdom tooth surgery image (man with jaw pain)

## Steps to Add Images:
1. Save all 6 images from your message to `public/images/` folder
2. Rename them exactly as listed above
3. The website will automatically display them in the Services and Before/After sections

## Image Specifications:
- Format: JPG (recommended) or PNG
- Recommended size: 1200x900px or similar aspect ratio
- The images will be automatically optimized by Next.js

Once you add these images, refresh your browser at http://localhost:3001 to see them displayed!
